An RPG-themed app to gamify your chores!

Add tasks for yourself to complete from the Tasks menu. Tap tasks to complete them and claim the rewards.

In the characters menu, create new characters for yourself and set an active one to work on.

In the shop, tap the shiny items to buy them with the current character's gold. View your hoard in the inventory screen.
